As someone deep in the job search grind myself, I kept jumping between five different tools — one for resume feedback, another for cover letters, a spreadsheet for tracking applications, LinkedIn for research... it was exhausting. I thought: why isn't all of this in one place?

It's also been my deepest dive into building end-to-end AI systems — from prompt engineering to full-stack deployment.
